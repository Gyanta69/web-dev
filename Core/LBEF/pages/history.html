Our History

1999

Foundation

LBEF Sports Organization was established with the vision of promoting sports excellence in college education.

2005

First Championship

Our football team won the first inter-college championship, marking the beginning of our success story.

2010

Sports Complex

Inauguration of our state-of-the-art sports complex, featuring modern facilities for multiple sports.

2015

National Recognition

Received national recognition for outstanding contribution to college sports development.

2020

Digital Transformation

Launched digital sports management system and virtual training programs during the pandemic.

2023

International Partnerships

Established partnerships with international sports organizations for student exchange programs.
